<p><p>Automotive metal die casting is a manufacturing process used to produce metal parts for vehicles with high precision and excellent surface finish. The shift towards electric vehicles is also contributing to market expansion, as these vehicles require specialized die-cast parts for battery enclosures and electric motor housings.
