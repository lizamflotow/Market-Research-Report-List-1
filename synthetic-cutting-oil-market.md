<p><p>The synthetic cutting oil market is categorized primarily into water-based and oil-based cutting oils. Water-based cutting oils primarily utilize water as a solvent, offering superior cooling properties and easier cleanup, making them ideal for high-speed machining. In contrast, oil-based cutting oils are formulated with mineral or synthetic oils, providing excellent lubrication and reducing tool wear, suitable for heavy-duty machining tasks. Both types aim to enhance machining efficiency and extend tool life, catering to different industrial applications.</p></p>
