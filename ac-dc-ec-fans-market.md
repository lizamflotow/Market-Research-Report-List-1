<p><p>The AC-DC-EC fans market encompasses various fan types, each serving distinct applications. Axial fans move air parallel to the axis of rotation, ideal for cooling and ventilation. Diagonal fans combine axial and centrifugal designs for improved efficiency. Centrifugal fans use rotating blades to push air at right angles, suitable for high-pressure applications. Tangential fans create a uniform air stream across surfaces, commonly used in heating and cooling systems. Other fan types include specialty fans for specific industrial needs, enhancing versatility in airflow solutions.</p></p>
