<p><p>The piezoelectric inkjet printer market is categorized into solid inkjet and liquid inkjet types. Solid inkjet printers use solid sticks of ink that are melted and then jetted onto media, providing vibrant colors and reducing waste. In contrast, liquid inkjet printers utilize fluid inks that are ejected through nozzles, allowing for greater flexibility and a wider range of media. Both types leverage piezoelectric technology to deliver precise droplet control, enabling high-quality printing for various applications.</p></p>
