One-sensor Doppler flow meters employ a single transducer to emit and receive sound waves, suitable for simpler applications. Two-sensor Doppler flow meters use two transducers for greater accuracy and reliability, making them ideal for complex or high-demand environments. Each type serves specific needs based on the precision and complexity of the measurement required.</p></p>
