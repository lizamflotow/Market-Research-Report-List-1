<p><p>The Production Monitoring System market is categorized into online and offline monitoring systems. Online monitoring systems enable real-time data collection and analysis via the internet, facilitating immediate decision-making and responsiveness. They often incorporate sensors and IoT technology for constant surveillance. In contrast, offline monitoring systems gather data manually or through local networks, ideal for environments with limited internet connectivity. Both systems aim to enhance production efficiency, minimize downtime, and ensure quality control but differ in their data accessibility and operation methods.</p></p>
