<p><ul><li>Solid Etalon</li><li>Air Spaced Etalon</li><li>Piezo Tunable Etalon</li><li>Gires–Tournois Etalon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Etalon markets comprise various types, including Solid Etalon, which is used for precise wavelength selection in optics, often in lasers. Air Spaced Etalon features a gap filled with air, enhancing tunability and stability. Piezo Tunable Etalons allow adjustable optical properties using piezoelectric elements, enabling precise control over wavelength. Gires–Tournois Etalon utilizes multiple reflections to provide high finesse for narrowband filtering, making it essential in telecommunications and spectroscopy. Each type serves unique applications in optics and photonics.</p></p>
