<p><p>The elbow orthoses market is categorized into dynamic and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics provide support while allowing movement, facilitating rehabilitation by promoting mobility and enhancing functionality. They are often used in cases where gradual recovery is needed. In contrast, static orthotics immobilize the elbow joint, providing stability and protection, particularly after surgeries or injuries. This type is crucial for maintaining alignment and preventing further damage, making both types essential for various therapeutic needs in elbow support.</p></p>
