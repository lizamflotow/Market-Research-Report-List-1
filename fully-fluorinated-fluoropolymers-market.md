<p><p>The fully fluorinated fluoropolymers market includes three primary types: PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), FEP (fluorinated ethylene propylene), and PFA (perfluoroalkoxy alkane). PTFE is known for its exceptional chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making it ideal for applications in various industries. FEP offers similar properties but is more flexible and transparent, often used in wire insulation and coatings. PFA combines the benefits of PTFE and FEP, providing better processing and flow characteristics, making it suitable for demanding applications in semiconductor and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
