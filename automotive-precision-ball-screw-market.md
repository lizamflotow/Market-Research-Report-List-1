<p><strong>The Automotive Precision Ball Screw Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Ball Screw</li><li>Internal Ball Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive precision ball screw market consists of two main types: external ball screws and internal ball screws. External ball screws feature a screw shaft that is externally threaded, typically offering higher load capacities and efficiency, making them suitable for applications like steering and suspension systems. In contrast, internal ball screws have a threaded nut located inside a cylindrical housing, which generally allows for compact designs and is ideal for space-constrained applications, ensuring smooth motion and durability in vehicle mechanisms.</p></p>
