<p><p>The photovoltaic pump market consists of several types tailored for specific applications. DC Surface Suction pumps are designed for shallow water extraction, utilizing solar power for efficiency. AC Submersible pumps operate underwater, ideal for deeper sources, powered by alternating current. DC Submersible pumps also function below the surface, leveraging direct current for energy-efficient operations in wells. AC Floating pumps are versatile, used in open water bodies, providing mobility and easy access to solar energy for various irrigation needs.</p></p>
