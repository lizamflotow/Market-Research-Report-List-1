<p><ul><li>Single Beam Scanning Sonar System</li><li>Multi-beam Sonar System</li><li>Side Scan Sonar System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sonar market types encompass various systems used for underwater exploration and mapping. Single beam scanning sonar systems emit a single sound wave, providing depth measurements for straightforward applications. Multi-beam sonar systems utilize multiple beams simultaneously, creating detailed, high-resolution seafloor maps. Side scan sonar systems offer wide-area coverage, capturing images of the seafloor by transmitting sonar waves to the sides of the vessel. Other market types include specialty applications like imaging sonar, used for object detection and navigation in challenging underwater environments.</p></p>
