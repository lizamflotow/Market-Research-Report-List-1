<p><p>The Pedestrian Protection System market is primarily divided into two types: Passive and Active systems. Passive Pedestrian Protection Systems focus on physical features designed to minimize injury during accidents, such as crumple zones, energy-absorbing structures, and safer vehicle designs. In contrast, Active Pedestrian Protection Systems utilize technology to prevent collisions, incorporating features like automatic emergency braking, pedestrian detection sensors, and advanced driver assistance systems. Together, these systems enhance safety for pedestrians by either mitigating impact or preventing accidents altogether.</p></p>
