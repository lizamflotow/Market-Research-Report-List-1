<p><p>Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM) are advanced imaging tools that use focused beams of electrons to produce high-resolution images of surfaces at the nanoscale. The market includes various types: W-SEM (tungsten filament SEM), known for its cost-effectiveness and simplicity; FEG-SEM (field emission gun SEM), which offers superior resolution and brightness for high-quality imaging; and FIB-SEM (focused ion beam SEM), capable of both imaging and precise material removal. Each type serves distinct applications across materials science, biology, and electronics.</p></p>
