<p><ul><li>Ordinary Dust Mop</li><li>Electrostatic Dust Mop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dust mop market is primarily divided into two types: ordinary dust mops and electrostatic dust mops. Ordinary dust mops feature traditional materials like cotton or microfiber that effectively capture dust and debris through physical contact. In contrast, electrostatic dust mops utilize materials that create an electrostatic charge, attracting dust particles for enhanced cleaning efficiency. While ordinary dust mops are cost-effective for everyday use, electrostatic dust mops offer advanced performance, making them suitable for environments requiring superior dust management.</p></p>
