<p><p>The domestic pressure booster pumps market can be categorized into two main types: single-stage and multiple-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed to deliver water at a consistent flow rate and pressure, making them ideal for smaller households or applications with low demand. In contrast, multiple-stage pumps utilize two or more impellers to generate higher pressure, catering to larger residences or systems that require greater water flow and pressure, such as multi-story buildings or irrigation systems. Both types enhance water delivery efficiency.</p></p>
