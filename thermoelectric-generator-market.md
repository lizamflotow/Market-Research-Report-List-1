<p><p>The thermoelectric generator market encompasses various types, each serving unique purposes. Waste heat recovery focuses on capturing excess heat from industrial processes and converting it into usable electricity. Energy harvesting involves drawing small amounts of energy from ambient sources, like temperature gradients, to power small devices. Direct power generation converts thermal energy directly into electricity, while co-generation simultaneously produces electricity and useful heat from the same energy source, enhancing overall efficiency and reducing energy waste across applications.</p></p>
