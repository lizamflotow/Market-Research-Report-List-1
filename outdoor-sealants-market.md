<p><strong>The Outdoor Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-borne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Hot-melts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Outdoor sealants are categorized into four main types based on their composition and application. **Water-borne sealants** are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and ideal for general-purpose use. **Solvent-borne sealants** offer strong adhesion and durability, suitable for harsh environments. **Reactive sealants** chemically cure upon exposure to moisture or air, providing excellent flexibility and weather resistance. **Hot-melts** are thermoplastic adhesives applied in molten form, offering quick bonding and high strength, often used in industrial settings. Each type caters to specific outdoor sealing needs.</p></p>
