<p><p>The humidifier market comprises several types, including ultrasonic and evaporative humidifiers, along with other variants. Ultrasonic humidifiers use high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist, providing quiet operation and energy efficiency. Evaporative humidifiers utilize a fan to evaporate water from a wick filter, naturally humidifying the air while also filtering it. Other types may include steam vaporizers, impeller humidifiers, and hybrid models. These diverse options cater to varying preferences and needs for indoor air quality management.</p></p>
