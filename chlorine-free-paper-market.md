<p><p>The chlorine-free paper market consists of two main types: Totally Chlorine-Free (TCF) paper and Elemental Chlorine-Free (ECF) paper. TCF paper is produced without the use of any chlorine compounds during the bleaching process, ensuring minimal environmental impact. In contrast, ECF paper utilizes chlorine dioxide instead of elemental chlorine, reducing harmful byproducts while still achieving a high level of whiteness. Both types cater to environmentally conscious consumers and industries seeking sustainable alternatives in paper production.</p></p>
