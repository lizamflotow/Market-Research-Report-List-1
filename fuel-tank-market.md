<p><p>The fuel tank market is categorized into three main types based on material: high-density polyethylene (HDPE), steel, and aluminum. HDPE fuel tanks are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and cost-effective, making them popular for various applications. Steel fuel tanks offer strength and durability but can be prone to rust if not properly coated. Aluminum tanks are lightweight and resistant to corrosion, ideal for high-performance vehicles. Each material provides distinct advantages, influencing selection based on factors like weight, cost, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
