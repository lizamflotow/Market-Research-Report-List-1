<p><ul><li>Thermal Type</li><li>Photonic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz Power Detectors market comprises two primary types: thermal and photonic. Thermal type detectors measure terahertz power by assessing temperature changes in a material when exposed to terahertz radiation; they are known for their sensitivity and broad frequency response. In contrast, photonic type detectors leverage optical properties to detect terahertz frequencies, often using nonlinear effects in materials. These detectors offer faster response times and higher bandwidth capabilities, making them suitable for advanced applications in telecommunications and imaging technologies.</p></p>
