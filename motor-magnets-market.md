<p><p>The motor magnets market includes two primary applications: Surface Permanent Magnet Motors (SPMM) and Internal Permanent Magnet Motors (IPMM). SPMM feature magnets mounted on the surface of the rotor, providing high torque and efficiency, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, IPMM have magnets embedded within the rotor, allowing for more compact designs and improved thermal performance, suitable for electric vehicles and consumer electronics. Both types play a crucial role in advancing energy efficiency and performance in various industries.</p></p>
