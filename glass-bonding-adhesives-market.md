<p><p>The glass bonding adhesives market comprises several key types, including UV curable cyanoacrylate, silicone, UV curable epoxy, and polyurethane. UV curable cyanoacrylate offers fast curing with strong adhesion for tight tolerances. Silicone adhesives provide flexibility and durability, making them suitable for various applications. UV curable epoxy combines high strength with resistance to environmental factors, ideal for structural bonding. Polyurethane adhesives offer excellent elasticity and are suitable for diverse substrates, ensuring a versatile range of applications in glass bonding.</p></p>
