<p><p>The hyperosmotic laxatives market includes three main types: Lactulose Type, Polymer Type, and Saline Type. Lactulose Type contains synthetic sugars that draw water into the intestines, softening stool and enhancing bowel movement. Polymer Type uses macromolecules to retain water and facilitate stool passage, often with a gentler action. Saline Type consists of salts that create osmotic pressure, rapidly drawing fluid into the intestines for quicker relief. Each type serves distinct patient needs and preferences in constipation treatment.</p></p>
