<p><p>The Smart Vision Camera market primarily features two types: Line Scan Cameras and Area Scan Cameras. Line Scan Cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for high-speed industrial applications and continuous moving objects, such as in conveyor systems. Conversely, Area Scan Cameras capture entire images at once, suitable for applications requiring detailed inspections of stationary objects. Both types leverage smart technology for real-time image processing, automation, and improved quality control in various sectors, including manufacturing and security.</p></p>
