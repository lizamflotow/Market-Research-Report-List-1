UIR Sensors operate without the need for cooling mechanisms, making them compact, cost-effective, and suitable for applications like smartphones and drones. In contrast, CIR Sensors utilize cooling systems for enhanced sensitivity and performance, ideal for high-precision applications such as military and industrial uses. Both types cater to diverse mobile technological needs, impacting various sectors.</p></p>
