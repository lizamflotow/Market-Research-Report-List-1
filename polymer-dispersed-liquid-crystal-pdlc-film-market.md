<p><p>The Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) film market is segmented into two main types: None-Adhesive PDLC Film and Self-Adhesive PDLC Film. None-Adhesive PDLC Film requires an external adhesive for bonding to substrates, offering flexibility in installation. In contrast, Self-Adhesive PDLC Film has a built-in adhesive layer, allowing for easier application on various surfaces without additional bonding materials. Both types are utilized for smart glazing applications, enhancing privacy and energy efficiency in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
