<p><p>The bowed dulcimer market is categorized into two main types: all solid wood and laminated wood instruments. All solid wood dulcimers are crafted from a single type of timber, offering superior sound quality, resonance, and aesthetic appeal, making them highly sought after by professional musicians and enthusiasts. In contrast, laminated wood dulcimers combine multiple layers of wood, which provides durability and affordability, appealing to beginners and casual players. Each type caters to different preferences, budgets, and playing styles.</p></p>
