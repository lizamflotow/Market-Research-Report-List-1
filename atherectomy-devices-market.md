<p><p>Atherectomy devices are specialized tools used to remove plaque from blood vessels to improve blood flow. Directional atherectomy devices utilize a rotating cutter to target and excise tissue in a specific direction. Orbital atherectomy devices employ a diamond-coated crown that orbits around the guidewire, effectively sanding down lesions. Rotational atherectomy devices use a rotating burr to grind away plaque. Laser atherectomy devices utilize focused laser energy to vaporize stenotic tissue, offering a less invasive option for clearing arterial blockages.</p></p>
