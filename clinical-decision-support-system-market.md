<p><p>Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS) can be categorized into several market types. A Standalone CDSS operates independently, providing clinical recommendations without integration into electronic health records (EHR). The EHR-CDSS type integrates decision support directly within electronic health record systems, enhancing existing workflows. The EHR-CDSS-CPOE market combines this integration with Computerized Physician Order Entry, allowing for seamless ordering and recommendation processes. Lastly, the CDSS-CPOE market focuses solely on decision support integrated with order entry systems, optimizing clinical workflows and improving patient care.</p></p>
