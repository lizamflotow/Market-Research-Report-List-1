<p><p>The biodegradable plastic bags market features several key types. Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA) is produced by microbial fermentation, boasting excellent biodegradability in various environments. Starch blends combine starch with other polymers, facilitating biodegradation while maintaining a degree of flexibility and strength. These materials cater to environmentally conscious consumers, reducing plastic waste and promoting sustainable packaging solutions across multiple industries.</p></p>
