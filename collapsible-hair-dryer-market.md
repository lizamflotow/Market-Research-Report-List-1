<p><p>The collapsible hair dryer market features two main types: centrifugal and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers utilize a fan system that creates a high-speed airflow, effectively removing moisture from hair quickly. They are often more powerful and suitable for professional use. In contrast, axial hair dryers generate airflow parallel to the motor's axis, producing a gentler, quieter drying experience. Both types offer portability and convenience, catering to consumer preferences for compact, travel-friendly hair drying solutions.</p></p>
