<p><p>The rubber waterstop market includes various types designed for construction joints in concrete structures to prevent water leakage. The **Dumbbell Type** features a central bulb with tapered ends, providing enhanced flexibility and excellent sealing properties, ideal for high-stress areas. The **Center Bulb Type**, on the other hand, has a prominent center bulb that effectively accommodates movement between concrete slabs while ensuring a watertight seal. Both types are essential for applications in dams, tunnels, and basement waterproofing.</p></p>
