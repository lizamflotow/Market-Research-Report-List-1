<p><p>The Personal Radiation Dosimeter market comprises different types: Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD), Optically Stimulated Luminescent Dosimeters (OSL), Radio-photoluminescent Dosimeters (RPL), and Active dosimeters. TLDs measure radiation exposure by trapping electrons in a crystal lattice, releasing light when heated. OSLs use similar principles but stimulate stored energy using light. RPLs employ glass or similar materials to measure exposure through luminescence. Active dosimeters provide real-time monitoring, often featuring digital readouts and alarms, enhancing safety for personnel in radiation-prone environments.</p></p>
