<p><p>The coding equipment market encompasses several technologies used for product marking and identification. CIJ (Continuous Inkjet) offers high-speed printing suitable for various surfaces, while TIJ (Thermal Inkjet) provides precise, high-resolution markings for smaller applications. TTO (Thermal Transfer Overprinting) is ideal for packaging materials, ensuring clean and clear prints. Laser coding utilizes focused light for permanent markings, often on irregular surfaces. Other technologies may include dot matrix and label printing, each serving specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
