<p><p>The planar magnetics market encompasses two primary types: planar transformers and planar inductors. Planar transformers are designed for efficient power conversion and dense integration in applications like power supplies and telecommunications. They utilize a flat design that reduces size and improves thermal management. Planar inductors, on the other hand, are used for energy storage and filtering in power electronics, offering low magnetic interference and high efficiency. Both types benefit from advancements in materials and manufacturing techniques, driving their adoption across various industries.</p></p>
