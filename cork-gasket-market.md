<p><p>The Cork Gasket market comprises three primary types: Neoprene Cork, Nitrile Cork, and Composite Cork materials. Neoprene Cork combines natural cork with neoprene rubber, offering excellent resistance to temperature and chemicals, making it ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Nitrile Cork, blended with nitrile rubber, provides effective sealing and durability in oil and fuel-related environments. Composite Cork materials integrate various components to enhance performance and flexibility, catering to diverse applications across industries such as construction, manufacturing, and aerospace.</p></p>
