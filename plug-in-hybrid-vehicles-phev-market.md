The market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Extended PHEV**: Primarily electric with a gasoline engine for long-range support. </p><p>2. **Parallel PHEV**: Both electric and gasoline systems operate simultaneously, optimizing performance.</p><p>3. **Mixed PHEV**: Combines features of both extended and parallel systems, providing flexibility in driving experience and energy usage, appealing to a broader range of consumers.</p></p>
