<p><ul><li>Three Roller Gate</li><li>Swing Gate</li><li>Wing Gate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic turnstile market comprises three main types: three roller gates, swing gates, and wing gates. Three roller gates utilize rotating cylindrical bars for access control, ideal for high-traffic areas. Swing gates allow for a smooth passage and are often used in facilities requiring enhanced security and aesthetics. Wing gates feature retractable panels that provide a secure entry while allowing for swift access, making them suitable for both pedestrian and vehicle entry in various environments.</p></p>
