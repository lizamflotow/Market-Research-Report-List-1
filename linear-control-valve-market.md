<p><p>The linear control valve market encompasses various types of valves used to regulate fluid flow. Gate valves provide on/off control with minimal flow resistance. Diaphragm valves offer precise flow regulation, ideal for corrosive fluids. Ball valves ensure quick, reliable shut-off with minimal leakage. Butterfly valves are lightweight and suitable for large volumes, using a rotating disc to control flow. Plug valves allow for smooth flow control and are easy to maintain. Other valves may include needle and check valves, catering to specific applications.</p></p>
