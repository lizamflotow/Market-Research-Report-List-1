<p><strong>The Organic-inorganic Hybrid Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EB Cured</li><li>UV Cured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic-inorganic hybrid resin market comprises two primary types: EB (Electron Beam) cured and UV (Ultraviolet) cured resins. EB cured resins utilize high-energy electron beams to initiate polymerization, offering superior cross-linking and chemical resistance, making them ideal for applications requiring durability. In contrast, UV cured resins leverage ultraviolet light to achieve rapid curing and hardening, allowing for efficient production processes with minimal solvent use. Both types cater to various industries, including coatings, adhesives, and electronics, enhancing performance and sustainability.</p></p>
