<p><p>The UV measuring instrument market is segmented based on the specific wavelength ranges of ultraviolet light. UV-B (280-315 nm) devices measure medium-wave UV, significant for studying sunburn and vitamin D synthesis. UV-C (230-280 nm) instruments focus on short-wave UV, primarily used in disinfection processes. UV-V (395-445 nm) tools monitor near-visible light, often used in various scientific and industrial applications. Other categories encompass specialized or niche UV measurement tools.</p></p>
