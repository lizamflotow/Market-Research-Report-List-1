<p><p>The Electrical Power Supply Transformer market is categorized into two main types: Stationary and Portable transformers. Stationary Electrical Power Supply Transformers are fixed installations designed for long-term power distribution in facilities such as power plants, substations, and large industrial sites. They provide reliable voltage regulation and load balancing. In contrast, Portable Electrical Power Supply Transformers are designed for mobility, allowing temporary power supply in construction sites, events, and emergency situations, where flexibility and quick deployment are essential for efficient power management.</p></p>
