Heavy Oil Burners, on the other hand, are designed for thicker fuels, common in industrial settings due to economic advantages. Dual-Use Burners offer versatility, capable of burning both light and heavy oils, appealing to users looking for flexibility and efficiency in various operational scenarios.</p></p>
