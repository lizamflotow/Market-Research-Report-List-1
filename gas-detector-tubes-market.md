<p><strong>The Gas Detector Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Gas Detector Tubes</li><li>Dosimetric Gas Detector Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gas Detector Tubes market encompasses two main types: colorimetric and dosimetric tubes. Colorimetric gas detector tubes contain reactive chemicals that change color in the presence of specific gases, allowing for immediate visual identification of gas concentration. In contrast, dosimetric gas detector tubes measure cumulative exposure over time, providing a broader assessment of gas levels. Both types serve critical roles in industrial safety, environmental monitoring, and health assessments, ensuring effective detection and management of hazardous gases.</p></p>
