<p><p>X-Ray Diffractometers (XRD) are analytical tools used to determine the crystalline structure, phase composition, and other properties of materials. The market is segmented into two primary types: Powder XRD and Single-crystal XRD. Powder XRD analyzes crystalline powders, providing insights into bulk samples' phase content and structures. In contrast, Single-crystal XRD focuses on individual crystals, offering detailed information about the precise arrangement of atoms within a crystal lattice. Both methods are essential for materials characterization across various industries.</p></p>
