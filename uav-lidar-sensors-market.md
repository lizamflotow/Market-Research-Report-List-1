<p><p>The UAV LiDAR sensors market is primarily divided into two types: solid-state LiDAR sensors and mechanical LiDAR sensors. Solid-state LiDAR sensors are compact and durable, utilizing fewer moving parts, which enhances reliability and reduces maintenance. They are suitable for various applications, including mapping and environmental monitoring. Mechanical LiDAR sensors, on the other hand, use rotating components to capture detailed 3D data and are preferred for high-accuracy applications. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, influencing adoption rates and technology advancements.</p></p>
