<p><p>Ball valves are essential in the oil and gas industry, offering reliable shut-off and flow control. Flanged ball valves connect to pipelines via flanges, providing strong, leak-proof seals. Threaded ball valves use threaded connections, making them suitable for smaller applications and easy installation. Socket-weld ball valves, designed for welded connections, are ideal for high-pressure applications, ensuring enhanced strength. Each type serves distinct operational needs, ensuring optimal performance and safety in oil and gas applications.</p></p>
