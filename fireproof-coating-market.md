<p><ul><li>Solvent Based Coating</li><li>Water Based Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fireproof coating market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based coatings. Solvent-based coatings offer high durability and excellent adhesion, making them suitable for industrial applications where extreme conditions are present. However, they typically emit more volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-based coatings are more environmentally friendly, featuring lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup. They are increasingly popular for residential and commercial applications due to their safety and ease of use, while still providing effective fire protection.</p></p>
