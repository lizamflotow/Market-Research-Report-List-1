<p><p>The UHP heat exchangers market is divided into two main types: metal shell heat exchangers and non-metallic shell heat exchangers. Metal shell heat exchangers, typically constructed from materials like stainless steel or aluminum, offer durability and high thermal conductivity, making them suitable for extreme temperatures and pressures. Non-metallic shell heat exchangers, often made from polymers or composite materials, provide excellent corrosion resistance and lightweight options, ideal for specific applications where chemical compatibility and reduced weight are essential.</p></p>
