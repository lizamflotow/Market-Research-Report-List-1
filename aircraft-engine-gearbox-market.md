<p><p>The aircraft engine gearbox market encompasses several types: </p><p>- **Reduction Gearbox (RGB)** reduces engine speed for optimal propeller or rotor performance.</p><p>- **Accessory Gearbox (AGB)** drives engine accessories such as generators and pumps.</p><p>- **Actuation Gearbox** manages power for flight control surfaces.</p><p>- **Tail Rotor Gearbox** specifically powers the tail rotor in helicopters.</p><p>- **APU Gearbox** connects the Auxiliary Power Unit to provide additional energy.</p><p>- **Others** include specialized gearboxes for unique applications in aviation. Each type is critical for efficient aircraft operation.</p></p>
