<p><p>The genome engineering market consists of various technologies that enable precise manipulation of genetic material. CRISPR technology offers a highly efficient and cost-effective method for gene editing, while TALEN (Transcription Activator-Like Effector Nucleases) provides specificity in targeting genes. ZFN (Zinc Finger Nucleases) allows for customization of genetic sequences. Antisense technology involves RNA strands that bind to specific mRNA, preventing protein synthesis. Other technologies include newer methods like prime editing and base editing, enhancing versatility in genetic modification applications.</p></p>
