<p><p>The automotive engine lubricant market consists of three main types: conventional oil, full-synthetic oil, and synthetic-blend oil. Full-synthetic oil, made from artificial compounds, provides superior performance, longevity, and improved fuel efficiency, ideal for high-performance engines.
