<p><p>Unipolar Hall switches are classified into market types based on their output configurations. Open-drain output switches allow for an external pull-up resistor to create a high output state, making them suitable for wired communication and interfacing with other logic types. Push-pull output switches provide both high and low output states, enabling direct connection to power supplies and reducing the need for external components. Other types may include variations in performance or specialized applications, catering to unique requirements in sensor and control systems.</p></p>
