<p><p>The heavy-duty truck steering system market includes three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), which utilizes hydraulic fluid to amplify steering effort, providing greater control and reduced driver fatigue; Electric Power Steering (EPS), which uses electric motors for assistance, offering improved efficiency and reduced weight; and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), combining both hydraulic and electric systems for optimized performance, adaptability, and enhanced vehicle dynamics. Each system contributes uniquely to operational efficiency and driver comfort in heavy-duty trucking applications.</p></p>
