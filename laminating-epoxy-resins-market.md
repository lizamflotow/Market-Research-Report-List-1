<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity</li><li>High Viscosity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminating epoxy resins market is divided into two main types based on viscosity: low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity resins are characterized by their fluid consistency, enabling easy application and penetration into complex molds and surfaces. They are ideal for intricate laminating processes. Conversely, high viscosity resins are thicker, providing enhanced structural integrity and resistance after curing. These are typically used for applications requiring greater strength and durability, making them suitable for robust industrial and aerospace uses.</p></p>
