<p><p>The Unmanned Aircraft Systems (UAS) market is categorized into two main types: fixed-wing and rotary-wing. Fixed-wing drones are designed for long-range, high-efficiency flight, making them ideal for mapping, surveying, and agricultural monitoring. They resemble traditional airplanes and can cover large areas quickly. Conversely, rotary-wing drones, such as quadcopters, offer vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, making them suitable for urban environments, aerial photography, and package delivery. Each type serves distinct operational needs, enhancing various industries' capabilities.</p></p>
